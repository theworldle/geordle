How To Play

GEORDLE is a spin-off on the popular WORDLE game for Geography Lovers.

Try to identify the COUNTRY and its CAPITAL using the letters on the Keyboard ONE AT A TIME. If the letter you selected is part of the COUNTRY or CAPITAL, the corresponding tiles will be highlighted in Green.

Each incorrect letter will cost you a Life. You have a total of 5 lives after which you lose the game. Lives Remaining are depicted using üî¥ on the Game Board.

This is the Practice mode, where you get unlimited attempts. Switch to the DAILY Mode, where you can play the actual game.

In this mode mode, your attempts are timed. There is a 5 sec time penalty for each life lost.
