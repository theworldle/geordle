How To Play

GEORDLE is a spin-off on the popular WORLDLE game for Geography Lovers. This is the Practice Mode, in the classic GEORDLE format, where you can refresh your knowledge of COUNTRIES and CAPITALS. Switch to the Daily Mode for the actual Game, which is in a different format.

Guess the COUNTRY/CAPITAL within the allowed number of tries. Hit the enter button to submit.

You dont have to match the Lengths when your Enter, as long as it is a valid COUNTRY/CAPITAL depending on the Mode. For eg: if you are playing the CAPITAL Mode and Capital to be identifed is ASMARA (6 letters), you could try SUVA (less than 6 letters) since that is a valid Capital. You cannot however, try INDIA (since it is a Country and not a Capital). Similarly vice-versa for the COUNTRY Mode. Modes are selected at random for Practice.

Use the Hint button, to reveal ONE letter at a time. There is a 5 sec time penalty for each use.

After each guess, the colour of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the answer.
